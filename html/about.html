About

I’m a medical student at the University of Calgary interested in multi-omics, gastroenterology & hepatology, and translational research at the interface of single-cell, spatial transcriptomics, proteomics, and clinical phenotyping.

Current focus: epithelial–immune crosstalk and host–pathogen responses in the gut, with emphasis on scRNA-seq, spatial transcriptomics, and protease biology.
